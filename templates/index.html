Equity Intelligence

Forecasting for S&P 500 Equities

LSTM deep learning, Hidden Markov regimes, and Monte Carlo simulation produce price predictions and trading signals on demand.

Analysis takes 2 to 5 minutes. The model is trained on demand against current market data.

LSTM deep learning

Recurrent neural network trained on years of NYSE and NASDAQ price history. Early stopping prevents overfitting.

Hidden Markov regimes

Identifies bull, bear, and transition states across S&P 500 names, attaching probabilistic context to predictions.

Monte Carlo simulation

40 forward paths over a 10-trading-day horizon yield expected price, volatility, and 95% Value-at-Risk in USD.
